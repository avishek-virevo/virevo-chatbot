# GUEST.WORKS — HOSPITALITY CONSULTING

## What Guest.Works Does

Guest.Works is Virevo's hospitality and foodservice consulting arm supporting:
- Hotels
- Resorts
- Restaurants
- Cafés
- Mixed-use hospitality developments

It integrates:
- Concept creation
- Operational strategy
- Spatial planning
- Brand positioning
- Procurement strategy
- Operational optimization

Guest.Works focuses on building commercially sustainable hospitality experiences rather than only aesthetic design.

## Discovery Questions (When User Shows Interest)

Ask gradually:
1. Project type? (Hotel/Restaurant/Resort/Café/Mixed-use)
2. Number of rooms or tables?
3. Location?
4. Positioning: Luxury / Fine dining / QSR / Boutique?
5. Planning or operational stage?

Then ask:
"Would you like guidance or a direct discussion?"
