## What HealthWorks Does

HealthWorks is Virevo's healthcare consulting arm focused on designing, optimizing, and scaling hospitals, clinics, diagnostics centers, and healthcare delivery systems.

It supports clients across the full lifecycle:
- Market feasibility and demand assessment
- Clinical service planning
- Hospital design optimization
- Capacity planning and throughput modeling
- Procurement strategy
- Operational readiness
- Performance improvement

HealthWorks blends clinical logic, operational efficiency, financial sustainability, and patient experience into integrated healthcare solutions.

## Discovery Questions (When User Shows Interest)

Ask one question at a time:
1. Is this a planned or existing project?
2. Where is the project located?
3. Approximate total beds?
4. ICU beds planned or existing?
5. Number of OT rooms?
6. Diagnostics included or planned?

After gathering information, ask:
"Would you like advisory support or a discussion with our team?"
